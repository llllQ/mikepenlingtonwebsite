About Me

Hi, I'm Mike Penlington.

I find that plenty happens early in the mornings. Bread making often commences before daybreak and many of my photos are captured around this time too.

My site combines my appreciation of Southern England countryside together with my passion for baking in support of charity.

I hope you get as much pleasure from looking at my photos as I gain during the moment they are remembering.
